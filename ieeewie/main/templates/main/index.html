About Us



Who are we?

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering. IEEE WIE envisions a vibrant community of women and men collectively using their diverse talents to innovate for the benefit of humanity.
